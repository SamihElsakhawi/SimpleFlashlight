Requested permissions

Depending on your device and OS version, " Simple Flashlight " may request you to approve certain permissions. Below is a list of these permissions and informations on why the app asks for these permissions.

 

Android.permission.CAMERA - Android.hardware.CAMERA - Android.permission.FLASHLIGHT:

" Simple Flashlight " needs access to your smartphone's camera, in order to use the flash.

 

Android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE:

Allows " Simple Flashlight " to access information about networks.

 

Android.permission.INTERNET:

" Simple Flashlight " needs access to internet so that it can display ads.
